## Setup your development

Learn how to update your docs locally and deploy them to the public.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your Pica Secret Key">
    Use the [Pica Dashboard](https://app.picaos.com) to create a new secret key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your Integration">
  
    Add a connection via the [Pica Dashboard](https://app.picaos.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install OneTool">
    Connect the tools from the Pica AI SDK to your LLM.
  </Step>
</Steps>
